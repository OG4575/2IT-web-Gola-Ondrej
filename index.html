Mark Zuckerberg

Co-founder and CEO of Meta Platforms, Inc.

Biography

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg was born on May 14, 1984, in White Plains, New York. He is an American businessman and philanthropist best known as the co-founder and CEO of Meta Platforms, Inc., formerly known as Facebook.

Zuckerberg attended Harvard University, where he created Facebook in his dorm room in 2004. The social media platform quickly grew to connect billions of people worldwide.

Achievements

  • Co-founded Facebook in 2004
  • Led the company through its IPO in 2012
  • Renamed Facebook to Meta in 2021 to focus on the metaverse
  • Established the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative for philanthropy

Personal Interests

Zuckerberg is known for his interest in artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and connecting people. He has also been involved in various charitable efforts.

Fun Facts

Mark Zuckerberg is married to Priscilla Chan, and they have two daughters. He is a proponent of open-source software and has donated billions to charitable causes.
